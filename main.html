Paris, France


Paris, the capital city of France, is one of the most popular tourist destinations in the world. Known as the "City of Light", it is renowned for its stunning architecture, rich history, and exquisite cuisine.

Top 5 Attractions:

  1. Eiffel Tower: An iconic symbol of Paris.
  2. Louvre Museum: Home to thousands of works of art, including the Mona Lisa.
  3. Notre-Dame Cathedral: A masterpiece of French Gothic architecture.
  4. Montmartre: Known for its bohemian history and the white-domed Basilica of the Sacré-Cœur.
  5. Champs-Élysées: The most famous avenue in Paris, known for its theatres, cafés, and luxury shops.

Must-Try Foods:

  • Baguette: A long, thin loaf of French bread.
  • Croissant: A buttery, flaky pastry.
  • Escargot: Snails cooked typically in garlic butter, chicken soup or wine.
  • Crêpe: A type of very thin pancake, usually with a sweet or savoury filling.
  • Coq au vin: A traditional French dish of chicken slow-cooked in wine.
